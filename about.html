About Us

Hey there!

I’m Cat. The Founder of The Content Catalogz. Also known as a Freelancer Marketer who focuses on analytics, strategy, and user behaviour.

I grew up hearing “dream big” but as soon as I did, I was called unrealistic. Told to be more realistic with my goals in life. But that’s never been me.

I came up with The Content Catalogz on a random Monday night when I was 48 tabs deep in my dissertation research.

Studying Business for a total of 8 years made me realise one thing: I wanted to be my own boss and run my own business. Over the years, I went through countless business ideas and ended up hating all of them. Until it clicked. I studied Business and Sociology during my A-Level years and always had an interest in the Sociological/Psychological factors of how we work and why we do certain things.

Marketing is exactly that.

It’s made up of scientifically curated algorithms that collect all of your data from your social platforms to create a feed that will keep you engaged and scrolling to your doom because your attention is currency and YOU are the product.

My mission sounds simple, but it certainly isn’t.

I believe that when it comes to Marketing, it’s a case of the Consumer VS the Marketing. I want consumers to become more aware of the tactics used across social platforms and marketing, and make more conscious, informed decisions. I want marketers to be more aware of the tactics they are implementing, the impact this can have on consumers, and be more ethical in their practices.

My goal is to help both sides make better decisions, but I also want to help businesses and creators reach their targets and grow their socials.

My promise to you, is to continue to learn, research, and develop strategies which are ethical, are tailored to your business needs, and help you reach your targets.

And no, I can’t make you go viral overnight, but I can help you implement strategies by analysing your content, social pages, and analytics.
